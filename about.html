Well hello hello!

I'm Michael Permyashkin. I live in Shippensburg, PA. where I attend Shippensburg University. I've been here most of my life. I graduated from Liberty University in 2013, moved to Arkansas for three years, met God, and moved back. I work full-time at my family's roofing company. Roof by day. Fiddler by night. I am literally the fiddler on the roof. My normal is goofy and speaking in dumb accents. I love songwriting. I am bad at covers. I could go on a bike ride everyday and be okay. That's the shallow version. Here's my full story.
