Hello

I'm Michael, but people call me Micsh, Permy or just Michael. Home is a small town in central Pennsylvania called Lewisburg. I grew up as the 8th of 13 children - proudly the start of the second half of Permyashkins.

I am in my senior year studying Software Engineering at Shippensburg University. I had plans of studying mathmatics and art in the hopes of becoming a teacher after school but I quickly found myself searching for something new.

Who knew that software was my perfect balance of creativity and problem solving?!

Enjoy a look into what I do.

What I do

Music was something I never thought I would be interested in learning. The summer after I graduated High School I began to teach myself the guitar and ukulele. Playing and singing my favorite songs eventually turned into writing original music. I released my single "Saved by Grace" in October 2020 on all platforms. My stage name is "Micsh."

Programming

I found myself in my first programming class my freshman year by accident. I understood the word 'keyboard'... and that was about it. I decided that I was going to catch up to everyone else so I began to code as much as I could. I became President of the Shippensburg Competetive Programming Team my sophomore year. Since my first programming course, I have come to love and appreciate the beauty of software. Projects have ranged from a functioning e-commerce platform, a 2D game, sorting algorithm visualizer and a full stack machine learning application.

My Music

I write music as Micsh (my name in Russian). Being my nickname since Middle School, Micsh seemed to fit my style.

I released my first song in January 2019 titled "Without You." Shortly after I decided to make a stylistic change and made the switch to Christian music. I released "Saved by Grace" in October 2020.

I think everyone needs a creative outlet that allows them to express who they are in a way that captures you. Music is my creative outlet. Writing is refreshing and has helped me find words to express my thoughts and feelings.

I hope you enjoy the music!

KnowLineCoffee

A progressive web application built with the Django web framework to view and update current line lengths at coffee shops at Shippensburg University. The application uses GeoLocation to determine if a user is within the proximity of a location before allowing updates to be submitted.

Once deployed, the application grew to over 600 registered users. Since Covid-19 restrictions have substaintially limited campus traffic, the application is no longer live.
